IBX System

The IBX System, Creating Nail Strength from Within. The IBX System is the first treatment of its kind that penetrates and toughens the nail from the inside out. It fuses the upper layers in the nail plate together to encourage growth and improve strength. The IBX System provides a protective nail shield under your preferred coating, (CND Shellac, CND Vinylux or CND Brisa Lite). It also helps promote natural nail growth. With IBX treatments you can grow your own natural nails and fix splitting, peeling, ridged, soft, breaking, and nails that never seem to grow. It can repair nails suffering from minor to severe damage Your polish look better and last longer. IBX can be used on its own or with your preferred coating. It should be used as an ongoing treatment to keep nails in a healthy state. Think of IBX as built-in insurance that simultaneously protects and toughens your natural nail.

Natural Healthy Nails

I am focused on creating beautiful natural healthy nails which is why I decided to use shellac and regular polish. CND Shellac is a true innovation in chip-free, extended-wear colour. CND Shellac goes on like polish, wears like gel, and can be gently removed in minutes. Shellac has revolutionized the industry and beauty professionals and their clients swear by it. Shellac has a zero dry time and a mirror-like finish that resists chips, nicks, and smudges. Nails are strengthened and enhanced with up to 14-days of durable colour. There is no need for drilling, filing or soaking to remove and there is no need to buff the nail plate to apply. It's not Shellac, unless it's CND Shellac.

I have helped my clients with weak breaking splitting nails to have beautiful long nails with the use of shellac manicures. I also offer shellac for nail bitters in order to help them grow their nails. This requires a commitment on their part to follow a planned course of treatment.

Hygiene

Hygiene is of the upmost importance. As I am a perfectionsit, I have to have everything clean in my salon and sanitary procedures are followed to the letter. Clients receive their own nail files, separate for hands and feet, buffers and foot files. After use they are placed in a bag with the clients name, one for feet and one for hands.
